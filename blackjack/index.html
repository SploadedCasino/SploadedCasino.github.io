How to Play Blackjack

In Blackjack, your goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible, without exceeding it. You are playing against the dealer.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are each worth 10.
  • Ace can be worth either 1 or 11, depending on which value helps your hand more.

How the Game Works

  1. First, place your bet using the buttons below.
  2. Click 'Deal Cards' to start the game.
  3. Both you and the dealer receive two cards. One of the dealer's cards is hidden.
  4. You can choose to 'Hit' to receive another card, or 'Stand' to keep your hand as it is.
  5. If your hand value exceeds 21, you 'bust' and lose the game.
  6. Once you stand, the dealer reveals their hidden card and continues to hit until they reach at least 17.
  7. The winner is the player with a hand closest to 21 without going over.

Special Rules

  • Blackjack: If your starting hand is an Ace and a 10-value card, you win 2.5x your bet.
  • Dealer Blackjack: If the dealer has blackjack, you lose unless you also have blackjack, in which case it’s a push (draw).
